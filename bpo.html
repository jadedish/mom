NIGHTSHIFT PROCESS

2
Select your menu items with the order form.

You'll get a link via WhatsApp to order from our menu, along with bank information to transfer your payment. We operate as a cashless business to ensure employee safety.

3
Pay for your order via bank transfer.

You'll get payment details via WhatsApp. After making the payment, send us a screenshot via WhatsApp to confirm your order.

4
Get ready for your delivery

Know your delivery window and plan accordingly. We'll message you when we're on the way and when we've arrived at your job site. We spend a maximum 10 mins on site. Have a backup person available to get your order and provide us their name.
