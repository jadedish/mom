CATERING PROCESS

1
Decide using our menu and prices

Note we price and package menu items according to the amount of people it feeds 'per person/serving'. This allows you to mix/match the menu items for any event and for how many you plan to feed.

2
Reserve your event date + Complete the online order form.

Complete our easy order form with the event date, delivery choice, your basic info and the menu items desired. Your order is placed on hold for 24 hrs while you submit payment and proof of payment.

3
Pay for your order via bank transfer.

Bank transfer info is supplied on the online order form and must be sent within 24 hrs. Once sent, send our team a screenshot of the sent payment via WhatsApp.

4
Get your order on the date you selected.

You can opt to pickup your order or pay for delivery. Details are provided on the online order form.

FAQs

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is your payment policy?
For our standard catering menu: You must submit payment within 24 hrs (1 full day) of submitting an order. Send your bank transfer confirmation screenshot to our team via WhatsApp 876-402-1047.

  • Full amount or total of order is required to finalize your order. Payment is required within 24 hrs.
For our wedding packages:You must submit payment within 72 hrs (3 full days) of submitting an order for our package. Send your bank transfer confirmation screenshot to our team via WhatsApp 876-402-1047.

  • 50% of the total for the wedding package selected is required to finalize your order. The deposit payment is required within 72hrs. The final payment is required the day before the event or date selected.
What is your refund or cancellation policy?
For our standard catering menu: If for any reason you need to cancel, fill out this form to request cancellation. Please note the cancellation rules below.

  • Cancel more than 48 hrs (more than 2 days) before event or date selected: 100% refund of total
  • Cancel within 48 hrs (less than 2 days) of event or date selected: 50% refund of total
For our wedding packages: If for any reason you need to cancel, fill out this form to request cancellation. Please note the cancellation rules below.

  • Cancel more than 7 days (more than 1 week) before event or date selected: 100% refund of total
  • Cancel within 7 days (less than 1 week) of event or date selected: forfeited deposit received (no refund)

What does 'Feeds' mean on the menu?
Good question! We used a measured approach to establish how much or for how many people our food trays that we provide to you will FEED. For a better idea see our measurements below. You use this to help you decide on how to share among your guests.


We know our people like to 'Mek Tings Stretch' and you're free to do that :). Our only recommendation is that on the day of your event, choose a measured (one size per food item type) sharing tool/utensil so you know your guests are being DISH'D equally.

Per person/ feeds 1 measurement:
  • For sides: rice, potatos, macaroni - 1 cup
  • For chicken: 1 Leg quarter
  • For goat: 1 cup
  • For festival: 3 festivals
  • For toss salad: 1 salad tong full
  • For steamed veg: 1 cup
  • For wings: 3 wings
